Simple Burger

This simple burger recipe involves seasoning ground beef with salt and pepper, forming it into a patty, and cooking it on a skillet or grill. Add a slice of cheese during the last minute of cooking if desired. Toast the burger buns, then assemble by placing the patty on the bottom bun, adding any preferred toppings, and finishing with the top bun. Enjoy your delicious homemade burger!

Ingredients

  • Beef patty
  • Salt and pepper
  • Burger bun
  • Cheese

Steps

how to make the burger

  1. Start by seasoning your ground beef with salt and pepper
  2. Heat a skillet or grill over medium-high heat and cook the patty for about 3-4 minutes on each side
  3. Now when you use the cheese, place a slice on top of the patty during the last minute of cooking to let it melt
  4. Once everything is ready, assemble your burger by placing the cooked patty on the bottom bun and place the other bun on top of it
